Laura Meaton
Laura is an actress who trained at the Royal Central School of Speech and Drama. Currently represented by Jen at Simon & How, Laura has been training and performing with the PappyShow since 2014.

Where are you from? Wales.
What's in your pocket? Headphones.
What would you do with the keys to the city? Make transport free.
Most over-used word? Right.
Biggest phobia? Eating noises.
What one thing would you take on a desert island? My sister.
Song of your youth? 'River Deep Mountain High' Celine Dion or 'Sultans of Swing' Dire Straits.
Best would you rather? Always have to say everything on your mind or never be able to speak again?
Best PappyShow memory? Probably the time I was assisted in running up the wall and flipping over and managed to bring the light fitting down off the ceiling...
