Simon Lennon
Simon is an actor and writer from Liverpool. He has been an active PappyShow member since it began in 2012. He trained with the National Youth Theatre REP Company in 2013 and has worked in theatre and television with companies such as the BBC, ITV, Almeida Theatre and the Ambassador's Theatre. Simon is also the co-founder of the new writing company, Itch + Scratch.

Where are you from? Liverpool.
What's in your pocket? My phone, a chewing gum wrapper and a pen.
Most over-used word? Like.
Biggest phobia? Being out of control of myself.
What one thing would you take on a desert island? Pesto pasta.
Song of your youth? Send Me On My Way - Rusted Root.
Best would you rather? Fight 100 duck-sized horses, or 1 horse-sized duck?
Best PappyShow memory? Either Sope Dirisu falling through a wall, during what I think was a variation of the game Tag. Or the closing sessions during week intensives. Safe space!
